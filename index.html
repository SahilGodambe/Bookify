A Game of Thrones

Author: George R.R. Martin

An epic fantasy that follows several noble families in the battle for control of the Iron Throne in the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros.

The Hobbit

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

A tale of adventure, where Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit, goes on a journey with dwarves to reclaim a stolen treasure from a dragon.

The Last Wish

Author: Andrzej Sapkowski

A collection of short stories introducing Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a Witcher, in a dark fantasy world filled with moral ambiguity and magic.

The Song of Achilles

Author: Madeline Miller

A poignant retelling of the Iliad, focusing on the bond between Achilles and Patroclus, with themes of love, loss, and fate.

The Silmarillion

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

A mythopoeic masterpiece that delves into the creation of Tolkien's world, the history of the Elves, and the origins of the great conflicts in Middle-earth.
